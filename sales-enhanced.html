Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know the products are authentic? +
All sellers on DeadStock Exchange are verified and rated by the community. We require real product photos (not stock images) and provide a 48-hour inspection window. If items don't match the listing, we guarantee a full refund. Our trust & safety team monitors all transactions.
What are shipping costs and timelines? +
Shipping is quoted at checkout based on item weight, destination, and carrier. Most items ship within 48 hours of purchase. We partner with FedEx, UPS, and DHL for competitive rates. Local pickup is available for many listings (free shipping).
Is my payment protected? +
Yes! Your payment is held in escrow through our Stripe integration until you confirm receipt of goods. You have 48 hours to inspect items. If there are any issues, we'll either arrange a refund or send replacements at no cost to you.
Can I return items? +
Items can be returned within 48 hours of delivery if they don't match the listing description. Simply contact the seller or our support team. Return shipping is covered by us. Refunds are processed within 3-5 business days.
Are there bulk discounts? +
Yes! Many sellers offer volume discounts. You can message sellers directly to negotiate prices for larger quantities. Our platform handles multi-lot purchases seamlessly with consolidated shipping options.
Do you ship internationally? +
Currently, we operate within the United States. International shipping is coming in Q2 2026. You can sign up for our waitlist to be notified when we expand to your country.

For Sellers & Partners

What's your commission? Can I negotiate? +
Standard commission is 7%. For large operations moving 10,000+ units, we can negotiate down to 5%. No upfront fees, no listing costs‚Äîwe only profit when you profit. Featured listing packages available for $50-$200 per item to accelerate sales.
How long does it take to sell my inventory? +
Average time to sale varies by category. Electronics: 5-14 days. Fabric/raw materials: 3-7 days. Seasonal items: Variable. Our platform provides an inventory age tracker + automated discounting rules so you can optimize pricing in real-time. Items with competitive pricing typically sell within 2 weeks.
Do I need to handle shipping? +
You pack and ship or arrange local pickup. We provide real-time shipping quotes integrated into the checkout. For bulk orders, our whitebox fulfillment service can arrange pickup directly from your warehouse (negotiable rates). You just provide tracking info and we handle the rest.
Can I upload 1,000+ items at once? +
Yes! Our bulk upload tool accepts Excel/CSV files with unlimited items. Auto-categorization, batch image uploads, and validation reports included. One upload can list 10,000 SKUs. Coming in Phase 2 (Q2 2026).
How does the inventory age tracker help me? +
Your dashboard shows exactly how long each item has been listed. Automatic alerts trigger when items approach 30+ days. You can set auto-discount rules: "Every 7 days without a sale, drop price 5%." This aggressive approach clears slow-moving inventory fast.
What happens if a buyer claims the item is damaged? +
Buyers have a 48-hour inspection window. If they submit a damage claim with photos, we mediate fairly. If you shipped properly (good packaging) and item was insured, you're usually protected. If you shipped carelessly or misrepresented condition, you may face a refund or replacement cost. Transparency = protection.
How do I get the "Verified Seller" badge? +
Complete KYC verification: submit business ID, proof of address, tax ID. Takes 2-3 business days. Higher buyer confidence = more visibility in search = faster sales. No cost, optional but highly recommended for B2B sellers.
When do I get paid? +
After buyer confirms receipt (max 50 hours after delivery), we transfer your payment within 2 business days to your connected bank account. Funds held in escrow protect both parties. No fees for payouts‚Äîwe handle all payment processing.
