Frequently Asked Questions

What types of inventory can I sell? ‚ñº

You can sell overstock, dead stock, closeouts, liquidations, and surplus inventory across virtually any category: apparel, electronics, furniture, beauty, industrial supplies, seasonal items, returns, B-stock products, and more. We accept inventory from retailers, wholesalers, manufacturers, and liquidators.

Not Allowed: Counterfeit goods, stolen merchandise, hazardous materials (unless properly sealed/documented), recalled products, or prohibited items.

Is there a minimum quantity to list? ‚ñº

No! You can list as little as 1 unit or as much as 10,000+. Most bulk buyers prefer larger quantities (50+ units), but we welcome all sizes. Our platform helps you find the right buyer for your inventory size.

How long does it take to sell my inventory? ‚ñº

Average time-to-sale is 8 days, but it varies by category:

  • Electronics: 3-5 days
  • Fashion/Apparel: 5-7 days
  • Furniture/Bulk Items: 8-12 days
  • Seasonal Items: 2-4 days (during peak season)

Speed increases with competitive pricing, professional photos, and detailed descriptions.

How much does it cost to sell? ‚ñº

Zero upfront fees. We only earn when you sell!

  • Standard Plan: 8% platform commission + 1% processing fee = 9% total
  • Premium Plan: 6% platform commission + 1% processing fee = 7% total
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing based on volume

No listing fees, no subscription fees, no cancellation fees. Just commission on actual sales.

How do I get paid? ‚ñº

We pay sellers quickly and reliably:

  • Payments processed 1-2 business days after delivery confirmation
  • Payout Methods: Bank transfer, check, or Stripe Connect
  • Schedule: Automatic monthly payouts or request payouts anytime
  • Minimum: No minimum payout threshold

You can track all payouts on your seller dashboard.

Do you handle shipping and logistics? ‚ñº

Yes! We coordinate shipping with your buyers:

  • Buyer arranges pickup from your warehouse or we coordinate drop-off
  • Discounted carrier rates through our logistics partners
  • Insurance and tracking included
  • Free return shipping if there's a dispute

You can also choose to handle your own shipping if preferred.

What if a buyer has issues with their order? ‚ñº

We have a comprehensive dispute resolution process:

  • Photo/video documentation of all shipments
  • Insurance covers damage in transit
  • Buyer protection with easy returns (seller covers return shipping)
  • Mediation team to resolve disputes fairly

Our goal is to protect both sellers and buyers. We enforce our buyer verification and ratings system to minimize issues.

Do I need to create a K YC profile? ‚ñº

Yes, KYC (Know Your Customer) verification is required:

  • Takes ~5 minutes to complete
  • Required information: Business name, address, tax ID, bank details
  • Improves buyer confidence and reduces fraud
  • Data is encrypted and compliant with data protection regulations

This is a one-time requirement per seller account.

Are there any restrictions on what I can sell? ‚ñº

We prohibit selling:

  • Counterfeit or inauthentic goods
  • Stolen merchandise
  • Hazardous materials (unless properly documented & sealed)
  • Recalled products
  • Weapons, explosives, or dangerous items
  • Illegal goods or services

Everything else is fair game! Check our full seller agreement for details.
